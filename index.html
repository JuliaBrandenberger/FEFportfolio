About Me

I'm Julia.

I'm a dance and theater artist who decided to start learning web development. I've discovered that I enjoy the mental challenge and feeling of accomplishment that comes from solving problems through code.

I've studied "How to Code Simple Data" and "How to Code Complex Data" through EdX with the University of British Columbia.

I was part of the Fall 2021 cohort of Front End Foxes.

I've completed the Foundations Track of The Odin Project, where I firmed up my understanding of Javascript fundamentals and made some of the projects listed below.
